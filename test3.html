INDIA!

India is the country of my origin. My whole family was born there except me.

India is one of my favorite places to go. During the summer, in Delhi, the temperature can get to 110 degrees Fahrenheit!

My cousins live in the suburbs of Delhi.

This page is just going to give you SOME information about India.

The capital of India is New Delhi. There are 29 states and 7 territories. India has the second highest population (1.2 billion) of the world after China.

There are 22 languages spoken all across India. The most common are Hindi (which I speak), Telegu, and Tamil.

The life expectancy is 68.5 years. The area is 1.27 million square miles. They got their independence on August 15, 1947 which is one day after Pakistan got their independence (research some stuff about the India/Pakistan partition).

India is the 7th largest country and is bordered by China, Nepal, Pakistan, Bangladesh, and Bhutan.

The most grown crops are wheat and rice, however Indians also grow pulses, tea, sugarcane, cotton, oilseeds, coffee, and rubber.

In India, according to the Koppen system India hosts the 6 major climate subtypes. It ranges from arid desert in the west, alpine tundra and glaicers in the north, and humid tropical regions in the southwest.
